About 5 Love Languages

    Word of Affirmation

    • Word of Affirmation are words that communicate with your love one. It is usually compliments or positive words or words that encourage them.

    Quality Time

    • Quality Time is about spending time with your love one. It is about giving the attention with each other and enjoying the time and moment together.

    Receiving Gifts

    • Receiving Gifts means that whenever you or your love one gifts one another, usually they would put their thoughts and time preparing. It is way for them to show that you are special to them or they are special to you.

    Acts of Service

    • Acts of Service is something that when you or your love one is doing something for you or when you are doing something for them. It is something like you are lending a hand or helping them with something.

    Physical Touch

    • Physical Touch is way to express such as a small gesture that can be meaningful between you and your love one like holding hands or hug.
